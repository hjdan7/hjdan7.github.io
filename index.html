Simpler times

Hi, I'm Henry. I play with blocks

I use 3D pixels, or voxels, to create vibrant 3D scenes reimagining the world around me and creating entirely new ones to explore. Voxels allow me to make expressive art that combines the nostalgia of classic videogame pixel art with beautiful 3D rendering to make something modern, emotional, and unique.

Hire me to help with:

  • Posters, Album Art, and marketing materials
  • 2D/3D Game assets
  • Recreations of your photos
  • Anything else that fits my style!
